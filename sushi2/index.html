About Us

Ami Sushi is located on the Southeast corner of Stirling Road and Pine Island Rd. Our friendly staff greets guests and the owners are hands-on and involved in the day to day operation.

We feature both a Sushi Bar and a Raw Shellfish Bar. We highlight the freshest, most delicious ingredients, and Seafood available in the marketplace in all of our dishes. Our fresh fish comes to us from all over the globe as we only deliver the best quality to and for our customers.
